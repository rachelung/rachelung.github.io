About

I work at the intersection of natural language processing and data science, building systems that understand language across cultures and contexts. My research spans multilingual style transfer, named-entity recognition, and evaluation frameworks for generative models.

Before graduate school I studied Data Science at UC San Diego and interned at WEBTOON Entertainment, working on user behavior analysis and large-scale data pipelines. I am drawn to problems where rigorous measurement meets real-world language diversity.

I am currently open to roles in machine learning engineering, applied research, and data science, particularly on teams working with multilingual or multimodal AI systems.
